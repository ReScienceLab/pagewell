# Skill: Launch Free Tool

## Purpose

Create a practical launch and promotion checklist for a newly generated free tool.

Do not wait for SEO. A useful free tool should get immediate distribution, feedback, and internal links.

## Required inputs

- free tool route and title
- target ICP
- product/company facts
- communities/channels if known
- analytics availability

## Output

Create a launch plan with:

1. Day 1 checklist.
2. Social post drafts.
3. Community targets.
4. Demo video script.
5. Internal linking tasks.
6. Analytics events to verify.
7. Week 1 follow-up tasks.

## Day 1 checklist

- Verify page works in production.
- Verify canonical, sitemap, robots, and schema.
- Verify analytics events.
- Post on relevant social channels.
- Share in relevant communities with context, not spam.
- Add internal links from product/use-case/docs pages.
- Record a 30-60 second demo video.

## Social post templates

### Short post

```txt
Launched a free <tool name>.

It helps <ICP> <solve problem>.

No signup. Instant result.

Try it: <url>
```

### Builder post

```txt
I built a free tool for <ICP> who need to <job>.

Why: <pain>
What it does: <utility>
How it connects: <product/category>

Try it here: <url>
```

## Community rules

- Be transparent that it is your tool.
- Lead with usefulness.
- Do not mass-post generic copy.
- Reply to comments and collect feature requests.

## Metrics

Track:

- page views
- tool uses
- copy/download/share events
- email signups if present
- product CTA clicks
- backlinks/social shares
- Search Console impressions and queries

## Acceptance criteria

- Launch plan is specific to the tool and ICP.
- It includes copy-ready posts.
- It includes analytics verification.
- It includes internal linking and follow-up tasks.
